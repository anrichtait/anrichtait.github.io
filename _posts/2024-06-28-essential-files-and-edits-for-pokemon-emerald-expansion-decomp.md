### Introduction:

While editing pokemon emerald decomps it can be quiet confusing at times to find all the relevant files needed to edit things in the game. This is especially true for the expansion repo that has a whole bunch of extra things.


This blog post aims to be a quick reference for myself and others of the most important files you will need to edit. I will update this list as I learn an discover more.
